    Simple Steak Marinade
    Homemade Meat Sauce

Ian's Flavorville

Shopping List

Measurement Ingredient
1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
1/4 Cup Worcestershire Sauce
2 Tablespoons Fresh Cilantro (Chopped)
1 Tablespoon Fresh Ginger (Chopped)
1 1/4 Pounds Flank Steak

Recipe

Step Instructions
1 Mix Soy Sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Cilantro and Ginger in a gallon freezer bag.
2 Add Flank Steak to gallon freezer bag, seal and evenly distribute marinade.
3 Refrigerate up to 24 hours.
4 Boil extra marinade and pour over cooked steak.
